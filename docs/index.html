Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate debts for my poker game?

Enter each player's buy-in amount and final cash-out amount. The app will automatically calculate who needs to pay whom and the exact amounts.

How do I handle game expenses?

You can record any game-related expenses (like food, drinks, or venue fees) and the app will include them in the final calculations, distributing them fairly among players.

Can I view my previous games?

Yes! The history feature keeps track of all your past games, including buy-ins, cash-outs, expenses, and final settlements. You can access this information anytime.
