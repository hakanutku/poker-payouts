Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Poker Payouts. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our application and tell you about your privacy rights.

Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Game data (buy-ins, cash-outs, expenses)
  • Game history and results
  • Device information and usage statistics

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Calculate and track poker game debts
  • Store your game history
  • Improve our application

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your game data on our servers. This ensures your privacy and data security.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data

Changes to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
