Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate payouts for my tournament?

Simply enter the total prize pool and number of players, then select your desired payout structure. The app will automatically calculate the payouts for each position.

Can I customize the payout structure?

Yes! You can create custom payout structures or choose from our pre-defined templates to match your tournament's needs.

How do I share the results?

After calculating the payouts, you can share the results directly from the app via email, text message, or social media.
